Splunk Tutorial Series - Part 2: Getting data into Splunk Enterprise

In this second video in a multi-part video series about Splunk, we will be talking about some of the different ways to onboard data into our Splunk instance.

We will go over all of Splunk Webs capabilities to ingest data, and talk about the Splunk Universal forwarder, what a Heavy or Intermediate Forwarder is, and when you might want to use the HTTP Event Collector or HEC in your deployments.

We will end the video downloading the Splunk Universal Forwarder .deb package onto our Apache webserver in preperation for our next video, where we will install the universal forwarder and configure some basic inputs for it.
